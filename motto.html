MOTTO

The precursor to the University of Jember has provided the foundation philosophy in developing the institution in the form of sesanti "RINARAS WORKS AMBUKA BUDHI, GAPURA MANGESTI ARUMING bawana"

The Sesanti bermakana:

WORKS RINARAS AMBUKA BUDHI is determined to strengthen the University of Jember to organize themselves, always work in harmony, harmonious and balanced which is based on faith and piety to accept the development of science and technology.

GAPURA MANGESTI ARUMING bawana a performance target of Jember University to produce graduates who Sujana as human beings whose devotion in society always carry the fragrance of the nation and the State, prosperity, well-being and peace of mankind

In addition to these sesanti in order to provide direction for improving the quality of input, process and output in a sustainable manner, UNEJ has formulated a policy of academic quality. Digest of the academic quality policy is UNEJ will always put quality (Quality First) .
